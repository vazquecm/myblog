Okay, so really...what am I thinking? Well to be honest, I have no IDEA!! Been a surgical nurse for about 20 years and now I just up and change careers. Crazy, I know! Sometimes you just have to do something different to find the "joy" in working again. So here I am...a student..a freshman in the computer world!

Surgical Nursing

At least I don't have to wear scrubs anymore and can actually wear some of the clothes that have been sitting in my closet all these years.

Becoming a Web Developer

Please don't ask me why I picked this field cause I haven't really got an answer. Saw an article in the newspaper and pondered for a couple of months before actually applying to school. So at least no one can say I am being to impulsive changing careers. Besides, I take care of my parents and this can turn into something that I can do from home and still make a decent living. Someone has got to pay the bills!!

Becoming a Freshman...Again.

I am now the freshman in my house. My son finds this concept extremely amusing, especially since he his graduating in May from Father Ryan. All I can do is laugh and shake my head at him. While I am learning to code apps and the web, Raphael wants to become a graphic designer. Perhaps one day we will work together.

Reasons to Change Careers

  • Less physical stress than nursing.
  • No 24/7 call anymore!!
  • Can wear real clothes instead of scrubs.
  • Spend more time with family
